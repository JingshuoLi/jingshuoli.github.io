I am an incoming PhD student in Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering at The George Washington University, where I will join the Flight Dynamics and Control Laboratory under the supervision of Taeyoung Lee starting in August 2026.

I am currently a second-year Master of Science student at University of Pennsylvania, majoring in Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics with a concentration in Robotics and Mechatronic Systems. During my master’s studies, I have had the privilege of working with Cynthia Sung and Nadia Figueroa on design and control-related research.

Previously, I received my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Boston University, with a minor in Business Administration & Management. During my undergraduate studies, I worked with Tommaso Ranzani on the design of soft actuators and end effectors for underwater robotic systems.
